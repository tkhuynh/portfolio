Viet Food Guru

There will be time you don't know what and where to eat. Viet Food Guru App will help you to find where to eat 15 recommended Vietnamese dishes. You can find the restaurants that sell a chosen Vietnamese dish from the list, or let the app randomly choose one for you. If you are still deciding, you can have a little chat with Mr. Guru. He will recommend you a dish from the list based on your answers. You can save the restaurant you decide to go to your profile and come back to write a review to share with other users.

Technologies Used:

Node.js, MongoDB, Mongoose, Express.js, Yelp API, jQuery, AJAX, Bootstrap.

Happy Weather

A pure HTML/CSS Javascript website which allows user to see current weather of user's location based on user's IP address or search weather of other location in the world. The application will show current weather status, temperature, humidity and wind speed. Location is display in Google Map. Next Five dates' tempertatures are shown in line graph for every 3 hours of the day. Two week temperatures are shown in clickable column graph to view more details (column with drilldown graph). Two week wind speed and humidity are shown in combined line and column graph.

Technologies Used:
Javascript, HTML & CSS, Bootstrap, JQuery, Google Map API, Google Geolocation, Google Autocomplete, Open Weather Map API, ipifo.io for IP location, Highchart, Mustache Templating
